How to Adjust Your Bike Gears

Follow these steps to fine-tune your bike gears for a smoother ride:

  1. Place the bike on a stand or flip it upside down for stability.
  2. Shift to the smallest chainring and the smallest rear cog.
  3. Use a screwdriver to adjust the high-limit screw on the rear derailleur.
  4. Shift to the largest chainring and the largest rear cog, and adjust the low-limit screw.
  5. Fine-tune the barrel adjuster to eliminate noise or poor shifting.
  6. Test ride the bike and make minor adjustments as needed.
